More to come

Milwaukee-based ink / ballpoint pen and digital artist showcases mundane and sometimes comical observations from the current social climate.

Deusclaw recently finished a 30-day illustration challenge which consisted on capturing objects around his and his girlfriend's one-bedroom appartment in Milwaukee. The illustration is intended to capture how these objects end up becoming more than objects as quarantine becomes longer, and we become more attached to their warmth and utility.

A documentation of Deusclaw's latest illustrations and sketches can be seen in his social media channels.

Social Media Prescence

The speed of instagram

I'm currently posting my work in progress, quick illustrations, sketches, and ideas to Instagram. The speed at which I can share my work using my phone is incredible and helpful to stay connected with my Instagram following and keep growing in this journey.

Behance Prescence

I've used Behance since 2011. It's a wonderful tool to have an online portfolio. It encourages to share more than one image and really provide a detail description of the idea, the process and the final result. It's something that I find very important to keep track of.
